Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

Yamamoto North America is committed to making this website usable for everyone, including people who rely on assistive technologies.

Our standard

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. The site is built with semantic HTML, full keyboard operability, visible focus states, and color contrast verified against AA on our dark theme.

Built-in controls

  • An accessibility widget (bottom-right) to increase text size, raise contrast, and reduce motion.
  • Respect for your system "reduce motion" setting — animations are disabled automatically.
  • Keyboard navigation throughout, including the menus and dialogs.
